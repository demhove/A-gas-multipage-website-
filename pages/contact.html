Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I start new service?

Call our new service line at 1-800-555-0199 or fill out the form above. We'll schedule a free consultation and provide a transparent quote. Most installations are completed within 5-7 business days.

What if I smell gas?

Leave immediately! Do not use any electrical switches, phones, or create sparks. Call our emergency line from a safe location: 1-800-GAS-LEAK (427-5325). We respond 24/7.

How do I read my gas meter?

Natural gas meters display your usage in cubic feet or therms. Read the numbers from left to right, ignoring any red dials. Our smart meters automatically report usage, but you can verify through your online account.

Are payment plans available?

Yes! We offer budget billing to spread costs evenly throughout the year, payment extensions for qualified customers, and senior/low-income assistance programs. Contact us to discuss options.

How often should I service my gas appliances?

Annual inspections are recommended for all gas appliances including furnaces, water heaters, and stoves. We offer comprehensive maintenance packages and will send reminders when service is due.

Can I switch from propane to natural gas?

Absolutely! Our conversion specialists handle the entire process including permits, line installation, and appliance conversion. Free estimates available ‚Äì most conversions pay for themselves within 2-3 years.
